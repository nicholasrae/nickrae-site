About

The Short Version

I'm a commercial pilot building a second career in aviation at 46. Training done, certificates earned, now grinding toward the flight hours that unlock airline hiring minimums.

But aviation is only half the story. I build iOS apps, run prediction market bots, publish study guides for pilots, and use AI automation to tie it all together. I taught myself to ship software the same way I learned to fly: by doing it, breaking things, and iterating fast.

Two decades of managing teams, budgets, and high-pressure operations gave me a foundation that shows up in everything I do now: CRM on the flight deck, product decisions in my apps, and risk management in my trading bots.
