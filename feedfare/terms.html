Description of Service

FeedFare is an iOS app that encourages physical activity by requiring users to walk a set number of steps before accessing selected apps. FeedFare does not actually block or restrict access to other apps at the system level. It provides a self-accountability tool based on step tracking.

Subscriptions

  • FeedFare offers optional premium subscriptions billed through Apple's App Store.
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.
  • No refunds are provided for partial subscription periods.

Health Data

FeedFare reads step count data from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. This data stays on your device and is never uploaded. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings.
