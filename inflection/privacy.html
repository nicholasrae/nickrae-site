Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Inflection handles information when you use our iOS app.

Information We Collect

  • Journal entries and progress data — stored locally on your device only. Not transmitted to our servers.
  • Subscription information — payment processed through Apple's App Store via RevenueCat. We do not store payment details.
  • Anonymized usage analytics — aggregate data to improve the app. Cannot identify you personally.

Data Storage

Your journal entries, quests, and progress are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Backup, this data may be included — controlled entirely by your iOS settings.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat — subscription management. Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — payment processing. Privacy Policy.

Push Notifications

Optional daily reminders. Enable or disable anytime in iPhone Settings.

Children's Privacy

Inflection is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
